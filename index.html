Notum remixed Kryptogen Rundfunk 2021-01-12

To celebrate a birthday of maestro Kryptogen Rundfunk, label kultFRONT asked a bunch of cool people to come up with dance remixes of his music. I dissected Blue Like A Carrot, which is one of my favorite tunes of all times in general. Making radio noise into something danceable is not an ordinary concept, and the resulting compilation, is absolutely wild as a consequence. It'll make you chuckle, contemplate your existence, and brock out on the dancefloor at the same time.

Notum participated in Alligator Gozaimasu, a collaboration between dozens of artists from around the world. Coil - Blue Rats (Notum Remix) out now 2020-06-17

I remixed one of my favorite Coil tracks, Blue Rats. Been wanting to do this for years and finally found myself in the right state of mind. What sounds like rats is a human, what sounds like whistles are rats.
